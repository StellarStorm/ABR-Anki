<h2>T1w requires a ??? TR and a ??? TE.</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>T1w requires a short TR and a short TE.</p>
<p>That is, \(TR \sim T1\) and \(TE << T2\)</p>
<p>Recall that MR signal \(S(TR, TE) = (1 - e^{-TR/T1})M_0 e^{-TE/T2}\). </p>
