## Question
<h2>What is \(N_{D, w}\)? What are the units?</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>\(N_{D, w}\) is the absorbed-dose to water calibration factor for an ion chamber located under reference conditions in a radiation beam.
[\(N_{D, w}\)] = Gy/C or Gy/rdg.</p>

</section>
