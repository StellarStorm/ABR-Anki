## Question
<h2>Relationship between FWHM and LSF</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>\(FWHM \approx \frac{1}{2 \cdot LSF}\)</p>
<p>So a FWHM of 0.1mm would correspond to resolution of approximately 5 line pairs per mm.</p>
