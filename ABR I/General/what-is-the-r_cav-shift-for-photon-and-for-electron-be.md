## Question
<h2>What is the \(r_{cav}\) shift for photon and for electron beams, for cylindrical/spherical ion chambers?</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>Photons: \(0.6r_{cav}\)
Electrons: \(0.5r_{cav}\)</p>
<p>Note that for a parallel plate ion chamber, no shift is needed.</p>

</section>
