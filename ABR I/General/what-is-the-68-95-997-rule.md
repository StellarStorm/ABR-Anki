## Question
<h2>What is the 68-95-99.7 rule?</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>In a normal distribution
- 67% of points lie within 1sd of the mean
- 95% of points lie within 2ds of the mean
- 99.7% of points lie within 3sd of the mean</p>
