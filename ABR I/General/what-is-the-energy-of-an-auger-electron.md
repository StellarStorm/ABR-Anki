## Question
<h2>What is the energy of an Auger electron?</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>\(E = E_{\gamma} - E_b\)
- \(E_{\gamma}\) is the energy of the characteristic x-ray
- \(E_b\) is the binding energy of the Auger electron</p>

</section>
