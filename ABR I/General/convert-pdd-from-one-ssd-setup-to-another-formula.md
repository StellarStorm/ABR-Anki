## Question
<h2>Convert PDD from one SSD setup to another (formula).</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>\(PDD_2 = [(\frac{SSD_2 + d_m}{SSD_2 + d})^2 \cdot (\frac{SSD_1 + d}{SSD_1 + d_m})^2]PDD_1\)</p>
<p>Note: the term in brackets is the “Mayneord F factor”.</p>
