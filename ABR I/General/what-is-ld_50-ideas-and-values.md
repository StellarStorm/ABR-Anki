## Question
<h2>What is \(LD_{50}\) - ideas and values.</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>\(LD_{50}\) is the uniform whole-body dose that would kill 50% of the population.</p>
<p>For young adults with no medical intervention, \(LD_{50} \approx 3-4 Gy\)</p>
