## Question
<h2>Specificity is the ability to ???. What is the formula?</h2>

## Answer
<section>
<p>Specificity is the ability to identify absence of disease, or the true-negative fraction. We can calculate it as
\(\text{Specificity} = \frac{TN}{TN + FP}\)</p>
